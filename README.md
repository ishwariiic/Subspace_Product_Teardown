# Subspace Product Teardown

Product teardown created for the Vocallabs.ai Product Intern assignment.

## What’s covered
- GTM &  Customer base analysis
- Trust & safety gaps
- UX observations
- Competitive risks
- Retention opportunities

## Approach
I used the Subspace website/app as an active user, explored onboarding flows, reviewed Play Store feedback patterns, and mapped observations to product/business impact.I also redesigned a few frames based on the research I did.

## Deliverables
- Final teardown PDF
- Supporting screenshots
